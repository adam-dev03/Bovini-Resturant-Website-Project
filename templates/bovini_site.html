Our Story

An Italian chef, a Lebanese kitchen, one table.

Bovini was born from a partnership between an Italian chef and his Lebanese wife, whose family recipes shaped the kitchen as much as his years training across Italy did. Alongside a close friend and business partner, they opened a small spot on Abu Dhabi's Corniche where both culinary traditions could sit at the same table — Italian technique, Lebanese warmth, and nothing lost in translation between them.

The Menu

Italian craft, Lebanese soul.

Bovini's kitchen blends Italian technique with Lebanese hospitality — wood-fired pizza, handmade pasta, and mezze made the way our founders learned them at home. The full menu is available in-house and changes with the season, so we're keeping this page free of a fixed list rather than posting dishes that might not be on the table when you visit.
